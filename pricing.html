Pricing

Fair rates, set by the community

We're actively conducting market research with tribal governments and institutions before publishing pricing. We'd rather get it right than publish numbers that don't serve the communities we work with.

Pricing coming soon

We're in active conversations with tribal governments and colleges to understand what sustainable, fair pricing looks like for institutions of different sizes and budgets. Published pricing will be available once that research is complete.

Why we're doing this the slow way: Most software vendors set prices for Fortune 500 companies and then offer tribal governments a "discount." We're doing the opposite — starting with what tribal institutions can actually budget for and working backward. That takes a few more weeks of listening before it takes a day of publishing.
