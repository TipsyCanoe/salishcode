Tell us about your work

Optional fields help us understand which departments and nations have the greatest need. The three questions at the bottom are what matter most — feel free to skip everything else.

The three questions that matter
1 What task takes you the most time each week that feels like it shouldn't?
Think about repetitive work, manual re-entry, chasing information across spreadsheets or emails.
2 What information do you find yourself hunting for that should be easy to find?
Grant deadlines, application statuses, member records, approval chains — anything that takes too many steps to locate.
3 If you could fix one thing about the tools or software you use right now, what would it be?
Could be a missing feature, a broken process, or just something that's frustratingly manual.

Responses are used only to prioritize tool development. We do not share or sell your information.
